POLICY GUIDANCE




The Policy Guidance assists companies and ministries in governments in order to maintain a certain etiquette when it comes to workplace management.

Things such as wellness of workers, public affairs and how the workplace or ministry is perceived or general rules and regulatations to provide a safe and healthy enviroment for the workers so they may draw in new workers and lessen loss of employees from undeseriable circumstances. Having these in place may not instantly fix all the issues in a working enviroment, proper implementation and penalization for not following the guidelines may result in a better future working enviroment.

This is an investment for companies to use and we provide that service as a Ministry.
