OUR MISSION


Together with stakeholders each day we work strenuously to a better future. We strengthen the workforce of Trinidad and Tobago Public Service by providing safe office accommodation solutions and enabling digital services.

OUR VISION


We are your trusted and recognised leader for public excellence service. We wish for Trinidad and Tobago service to become much more user friendly.

HISTORY

TIMELINE


1992


The Public Administration was established as a division in the office of the Prime Minister.
The Minister was Gordon Draper.
The Permanent Secretary was Ainsley Tim Pow.


2000


The Public Administration was dissolved


2002


The Public Administration was reconstituted.
The Minister was Senator Dr. Lenny Saith.
The Permanent Secretary was Jacqueline Wilson & Cillian Macintyre.
The Deputy Permanent Secretary was Cillian Macintyre & Myrana Thompson.


2016


The Public Administration merged with the Ministry of Public Communications.
The Minister was Maxie Cuffie, M.P.
The Permanent Secretary was Richard Maradray.
The Deputy Permanent Secretary was Kurt Mayer.


2018

The Public Administration separated from the Ministry of Public Communications.
The Minister was Mariene McDonald.
The Permanent Secretary was Joan Mendez.
The Deputy Permanent Secretary was Claudelle McKellar.


2023

The Minister was Senator Allyson West.
The Permanent Secretary was Claudelle McKellar.
The Deputy Permanent Secretary was Angela Lum-Joseph.
